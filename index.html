School Technology Project

This presentation is for our school showcase for parents, not investors. We demonstrate how technology can recreate music using an ESP32 system.

Song 1: Clocks - Coldplay

We first recreate the song “Clocks” by Coldplay using programmed frequencies and ESP32-controlled buzzers.

Why ESP32?

We switched from Arduino to ESP32 because it gives better timing precision and stability, which is critical for music synchronization.

The ESP32 acts as the “brain” of the system, sending exact timed signals to recreate notes.

📌 The ESP32 board above controls all timing, frequency output, and synchronization between the buzzers that generate the sound.

How It Works

Each musical note is converted into a frequency (Hz). The ESP32 plays these frequencies in sequence to form a song.
