🎮 Interactive Buzzer Simulation

By Benjamin & Kfir

It started with a question.

Can we turn simple buzzers into something… musical?

So we tried.

We took 4 buzzers. We connected them to an Arduino. And we started experimenting.

Then… it worked.

But how does it work?

Step 1

We created 92 variables. Each one represents a musical note.

The Logic

Every note has a name:

Letter + Number (like a piano) + N (normal) or S (sharp)

Step 2

The Arduino sends signals to the buzzers:

▶ Play a note ⏸ Pause ⏹ Stop

Step 3

The buzzers work together to recreate real music.
